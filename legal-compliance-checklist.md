# ZCOP System Legal Compliance Checklist

## Overview
This checklist ensures the ZCOP system meets legal and regulatory requirements before commercial release. Each item must be verified and approved by the legal team.

## Data Protection & Privacy

### GDPR Compliance (EU)
- [ ] Lawful basis for processing identified
- [ ] Data subject rights implemented (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
- [ ] Consent management system in place
- [ ] Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) completed
- [ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA) templates ready
- [ ] EU Standard Contractual Clauses incorporated (if applicable)

### CCPA/CPRA Compliance (California)
- [ ] Consumer rights disclosures provided
- [ ] Opt-out mechanism for sale/sharing of personal information
- [ ] Right to know and deletion requests handled
- [ ] Sensitive personal information provisions addressed

### Other Jurisdictions
- [ ] Local privacy laws researched and addressed
- [ ] International data transfer mechanisms established
- [ ] Age verification and parental consent for minors

## Terms of Service & Legal Documents

### User Agreements
- [ ] Terms of Service reviewed and finalized
- [ ] Privacy Policy compliant with applicable laws
- [ ] Acceptable Use Policy established
- [ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA) available for enterprise customers
- [ ] Service Level Agreement (SLA) defined

### Intellectual Property
- [ ] Trademark searches completed
- [ ] Patent landscape reviewed
- [ ] Open source license compliance verified
- [ ] Third-party IP clearances obtained

## Industry Regulations

### Security Standards
- [ ] SOC 2 Type II compliance (if required by customers)
- [ ] ISO 27001 certification path defined
- [ ] NIST Cybersecurity Framework alignment
- [ ] OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities addressed

### Sector-Specific Regulations
- [ ] HIPAA compliance (if handling health data)
- [ ] PCI DSS compliance (if handling payment data)
- [ ] SOX compliance (if applicable)
- [ ] FINRA regulations (if financial services)

## Risk Assessment

### Data Breach Preparedness
- [ ] Incident response plan established
- [ ] Breach notification procedures defined
- [ ] Forensic investigation capabilities in place
- [ ] Legal counsel contact information updated

### Liability & Insurance
- [ ] Professional liability insurance confirmed
- [ ] Cyber liability insurance coverage verified
- [ ] Indemnification clauses reviewed
- [ ] Limitation of liability provisions validated

## Governance & Documentation

### Records Management
- [ ] Data mapping and inventory completed
- [ ] Retention policies established
- [ ] Deletion procedures documented
- [ ] Audit trails maintained

### Compliance Monitoring
- [ ] Ongoing compliance monitoring procedures
- [ ] Regular compliance assessments scheduled
- [ ] Training programs for staff
- [ ] Policy updates and maintenance procedures

## Final Verification

### Pre-Launch Checklist
- [ ] Legal review completed by qualified attorney
- [ ] All compliance gaps remediated
- [ ] Documentation archived and accessible
- [ ] Responsible personnel trained
- [ ] Monitoring systems operational
- [ ] Emergency procedures tested

### Sign-off Requirements
- [ ] Legal counsel approval
- [ ] Privacy officer sign-off
- [ ] Security officer validation
- [ ] Executive approval for launch

## Maintenance Requirements
- [ ] Annual compliance review scheduled
- [ ] Quarterly policy updates performed
- [ ] Semi-annual risk assessment conducted
- [ ] Continuous monitoring dashboard established
